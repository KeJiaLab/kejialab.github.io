Hailin Pan

I'm majoring in basic psychology at Hangzhou Normal University. I joined Researcher Jia Ke's lab
in November 2023 as a Research Assistant. My research interests focus on using psychophysics
and functional magnetic resonance (fMRI) to investigate cognitive processes such as perception
and attention and their neural mechanisms. In my free time , I am also interested in fitness and travel.
