Research Professor Ke Jia mainly study the cognitive mechanism of human visual perception and working memory,
using the methods of psychological physics, ultra-high-field fMRI, neural modification, computational modeling etc.
His researches are published on Current biology, Annual Review of Vision Science, Elife, Journal of Neuroscience etc.
