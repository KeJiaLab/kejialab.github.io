Baoyu Wu

I received my Bachelor's degree from Henan Normal University and Master's degree from Liaoning Normal University.
In 2022, I joined the Vision Lab as a PhD student, where my research interest is to study the neural mechanisms of
visual perceptual learning using nuclear magnetic resonance technology. Outside of the lab, I enjoy reading books,
watching movies, strolling around the West Lake.
